About Us

Welcome to the official website of Last Common Ancestor. We are a group of Mizzou students and alum who came together to make music. We have been playing together since 2024 and have been performing at various venues in Columbia, MO. Our music is a blend of 90s-2000s rock, shoegazey and psychedlic influences. We are currently working on our debut album.
